1. Setting Full Element Opacity (opacity: 0.1;)

The opacity property controls the transparency of the entire element and its content. A value of 0.3 is mostly transparent.

Box with Opacity 0.1 (Background and Text are faded)

2. Full Opacity (1.0) vs. Full Transparency (0.0)

The range is from 1.0 (fully visible) to 0.0 (completely invisible, though still occupying space).

Opacity 1.0 (Fully Visible)
Opacity 0.0 (Completely Invisible)

3. opacity vs. rgba() Background Transparency

rgba() transparency only affects the background color, keeping the text fully visible. opacity affects everything, including text and nested elements.

RGBA Background (Text is Opaque)
Full Opacity 0.5 (Text is Transparent)
