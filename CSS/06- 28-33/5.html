1. list-style-type for Unordered Lists

Controls the shape of the marker for <ul> elements.

  • Disc (Default)
  • Item Two
  • Square Marker
  • Item Four

2. list-style-type for Ordered Lists

Controls the numbering/sequencing style for <ol> elements.

  1. Roman Numerals
  2. Item Two
  1. Uppercase Letters
  2. Item Four

3. list-style-position (inside vs. outside)

Outside (default) means wrapped text aligns with the item. Inside means wrapped text aligns with the marker.

Outside bullets doesn't take li style like background-color:cyan Inside bullets take li style like background-color:cyan

  • Outside position: Marker is outside the content box, allowing wrapped text to align neatly under the main line.
  • Inside position: Marker is part of the content box, and wrapped text aligns under the marker itself.
