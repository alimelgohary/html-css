Why build your own framework?

  • Speed: Once your utilities are built, you can code complex layouts directly in HTML without touching CSS [00:00:31].
  • Consistency: Ensures that margins, paddings, and colors are identical across all pages [00:08:36].
  • Future Proofing: If you decide to change your "Circle" size or "Red" color later, you only change it in one place in your framework file [00:09:01].
